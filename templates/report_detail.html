Definitions and Remarks

  • %REC — Percent Recovery
  • MRL — The minimum levels, concentrations, or quantities of a target variable that can be reported with a specified degree of confidence (also known as LOQ).
  • ND — Not detected at or above the MRL (or MDL if reported).
  • RPD — Relative Percent Difference
  • MS1 — MS/MSD recoveries outside limits due to source sample concentration > 4× spike; surrogate recoveries within criteria.
